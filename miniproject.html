Historical Places in India

TajMahal

One of the most famous historical sites in India, the Taj Mahal of Agra dates back to the 17th century. It was built by Shah Jahan, the Mughal emperor.

Agra Fort

One of the oldest historical places in India is the Agra Fort, located in Uttar Pradesh. A true example of the stunning Mughal form of architecture.

Charminar

Amongst the most famous historical places in India, Hyderabad’s Charminar is a mosque that dates back to the 16th century.

Qutub Minar

Delhi’s Qutub Minar ranks amongst the top 10 historical places in India. A UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Qutub Minar.

Fatehpur Sikri

An architectural gem and one of the most exquisite historical places in India is Fatehpur Sikri, a town in the Agra district of Uttar Pradesh.

Meenakshi Amman Temple

Built in the Dravidian style of architecture, the Meenakshi Amman Temple ranks as one of the most beautiful ancient places in India.
